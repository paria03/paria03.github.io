ReCraft

Languages: JavaScript, HTML, CSS
Technologies: AI-Powered Image Ideas, Sustainability Search, Web Development
View on GitHub

ReCraft is a hackathon project built during my final DonsHack as an undergraduate at the University of San Francisco, where our team won 1st place. The event theme was Environmental Tech, and our goal was to encourage sustainability by helping people discover creative ways to reuse unwanted items instead of throwing them away.

With ReCraft, users upload a photo of an object and receive visual ideas for repurposing it, along with step-by-step guidance and tutorial links from platforms like YouTube, Google, and Pinterest. The project was designed to make sustainable choices more accessible, practical, and inspiring.

Highlights include:

  • Photo-based idea generation for repurposing unwanted household items
  • Step-by-step reuse guidance to help users act on sustainable ideas
  • Tutorial discovery through YouTube, Google, and Pinterest links
  • Hackathon team collaboration under a fast-paced product and design cycle

This project reflects my interest in using technology to support practical, user-friendly sustainability tools.
